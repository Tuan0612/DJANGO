TH True Milk - A Commitment to Nutrition and Growth

TH True Milk, founded by visionary Thai Huong, is centered around the development of physical and intellectual capabilities as a strategic investment in the nation's future. For over 13 years, TH True Milk has pioneered in producing healthful, natural products and has actively collaborated with the government, educational sectors, and various organizations to implement long-term programs such as School Milk, School Health Program, and Vietnamese Nutrition Project aiming to enhance the stature and physical health of future generations.

Since its inception in 2012, TH True Milk has achieved remarkable milestones, including establishing the largest and most modern dairy farm in Southeast Asia and becoming the largest dairy enterprise in Vietnam with a herd of 45,000 cows. The company's efforts have been recognized internationally, earning multiple awards for excellence in dairy products.
